Research Interests

Mathematical Quantum Physics Nonlinear PDEs Bose–Einstein Condensation Dynamical Systems

My interests lie in mathematical quantum mechanics, nonlinear PDEs, and dynamical systems. Working with Dr. Nguyen Dinh Thi, I study Bose–Einstein condensates and related quantum many-body models, especially in rotating, self-gravitating, and semi-relativistic settings. I enjoy exploring how elegant mathematical ideas help explain rich and fascinating physical phenomena.
